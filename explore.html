About Family Coffee

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiasts Maria and Carlos, Filtro Coffee began as a small roastery in the heart of the city. Our passion for exceptional coffee drove us to source the finest beans from sustainable farms around the world.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of coffee lovers daily, maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the perfect cup of coffee that powers your day.
